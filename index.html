Nutrition Information

Coconut is generally regarded as a source of healthy fat.The meat contains protein and fiber, as well as some essesntial minerals such as :

  • Iron
  • Manganese
  • Copper
  • Magnesium

Historically people have used coconut as a part of traditional medicine.The meat is said to, among other things:

  • Counteract some poisons
  • Protect against disease
  • Ease inflammation
  • Kills bacteria

Uses of Coconut

  • Coconut oil is extracted from dried coconuts.
  • Coconut is used in cosmetic Industries
  • Coconut shell is used for manufacturing Activated carbon
  • Coconut is excellent source of Minerals
  • Coconut shells are used to make Handicraft items
  • Used in famous dishes like Chutney, Sambar and curries
